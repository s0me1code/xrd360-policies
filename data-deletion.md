# Data Deletion Request for XRD360

If you want us to delete personal data associated with your account, submit a data deletion request.

## How to request data deletion
Send an email to `support@xrd360.com` with:
- Subject: `Data Deletion Request`
- Your registered phone number or email
- Any specific data scope (optional)

## Processing timeline
Verified data deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

## Possible exceptions
We may retain limited data if required by law, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate compliance obligations.

## Contact
Support email: `support@xrd360.com`  
Replace this email with your real support contact if needed.
