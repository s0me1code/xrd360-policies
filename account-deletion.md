# Account Deletion for XRD360

You can request account deletion at any time.

## How to request account deletion
Send an email to `support@xrd360.com` with:
- Subject: `Account Deletion Request`
- Your registered phone number or email

## What happens after request
- We verify account ownership
- Your account is scheduled for deletion
- Access to the app account is removed

## Processing timeline
Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days after verification.

## Data that may be retained
Some records may be retained when required by law, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or security obligations.

## Contact
Support email: `support@xrd360.com`  
Replace this email with your real support contact if needed.
